A Simple Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

A very basic macaroni and cheese recipe


Ingredients:

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk, warmed
  • 1-2 lb sharp cheddar cheese

Steps:
  1. Boil and drain macaroni per box instructions.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet.
  3. Whisk in warmed milk and let cook until thickened.
  4. Add cheese to sauce.
  5. Add sauce to pasta.
